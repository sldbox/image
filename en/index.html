◈ WHAT IS SLD NEXUS

SLD Nexus is an interactive reference tool for the StarCraft "Lost Dragon" custom map. It provides two core features: a Hidden Combo Database for tracing unit lineages and calculating material costs, and a Daily Essence Calculator for tracking hidden unit completion across Korhal, Aiur, and Zerus factions.

◈ SCREEN LAYOUT

Left Panel
Grade Filter & Cost
Filter units by grade. See real-time material costs as you search.
Center Panel
Lineage Tree
Full recipe tree for selected units. Expandable nodes.
Right Panel
Unit Codex
Browse all Hidden/Legend units by race category.

◈ QUICK NAVIGATION

  • Use the ⚔ Hidden Combo tab to research unit recipes and material costs.
  • Use the ◈ Daily Essence tab to calculate your daily essence score.
  • Click HOW TO USE in the top-right to return here anytime.
  • Click RESET ALL to clear all selections and return to default.

◈ SEARCH BAR

  • Type a unit name in the search bar at the top-left. Example: Nova
  • To search multiple units: separate with , or /. Example: Nova, Amon
  • To specify quantity: use UnitName*Qty. Example: Tychus Findlay*2
  • Colors on unit names indicate their grade. Cost dashboard updates automatically.

◈ LINEAGE TREE

  • The center panel shows the full crafting tree for searched units.
  • Click any unit badge to expand/collapse its sub-tree.
  • Use Expand All / Collapse All to view or hide all branches at once.
  • Materials: shows the direct recipe. Magic Cost: shows the total base unit breakdown.

◈ GRADE FILTER

  • Click any grade button (Magic, Rare, Epic…) on the left panel to see all units of that grade.
  • Click any unit in the result list to instantly load its lineage tree.

◈ UNIT CODEX (RIGHT PANEL)

  • Browse hidden and legend units organized by race: T-Bio T-Mech P-Bio P-Mech Zerg Hybrid
  • Click any unit card to load its lineage in the center panel.
  • Units are listed in order: Super Hidden → Hidden → Legend

◈ WHAT IS DAILY ESSENCE

Each Hidden or Super Hidden unit you successfully craft in-game earns Essence points. These accumulate into Korhal, Aiur, and Zerus essence scores, which provide buffs or rewards. The Daily Essence tab lets you track which combos you've completed today.

◈ HOW TO USE

  • Click unit buttons in the Korhal, Aiur, Zerus, and Hybrid groups to mark them as completed.
  • Hybrid units count toward ALL three factions simultaneously.
  • The score circle at the top updates in real-time.
  • Click Select All in each group to mark everything in that group.
  • Click Reset (Deselect All) to clear all selections.
  • Click a unit button on the left to see its lineage on the right.

◈ ESSENCE SCORING

  • Each Hidden/Super Hidden unit from Terran = +1 Korhal Essence
  • Each Hidden/Super Hidden unit from Protoss = +1 Aiur Essence
  • Each Hidden/Super Hidden unit from Zerg = +1 Zerus Essence
  • Each Hybrid unit = +1 to ALL three factions

◈ UNIT GRADE SYSTEM

Units in SLD are tiered by rarity and crafting complexity. Higher grades typically require multiple ingredients and specific conditions.

[M] Magic
Base units. Summoned directly.
[R] Rare
Simple 2-ingredient combos.
[E] Epic
Mid-tier crafted units.
[U] Unique
Cross-race combinations.
[HE] Hell
High-cost powerful units.
[L] Legend
Top-tier regular units.
[H] Hidden
Secret combos. Grants Essence.
[S] Super Hidden
Rarest units. SXD+ conditions required.

◈ GRADE ABBREVIATIONS IN RECIPES

  • SS+ — Star Star or higher upgrade level
  • SSS+ — Three-Star or higher
  • X+ — Awakened (X grade) or higher
  • XD+ — Double-Awakened or higher
  • SXD+ — Super Double-Awakened (highest)
  • 300%+ — Unit must be at 300% HP or higher
  • 300kills+ — Unit must have 300 or more kills
  • Lv7 — Unit must be exactly Level 7
  • Limit Break 3+ — Unit has broken limits 3 or more times
